**Leader(s):** [[Tiamat]]

**Goals/Motives:** Gather treasure from across the countryside to build a grand hoard meant to herald [[Tiamat]]’s return; free her from the Nine Hells; restore her power so she can enter the Material Plane, overthrow the gods, and plunge the world into chaos

**Allies:** [[Kobolds|kobolds]], black-robed cultists, other servants of the [[Cult of the Dragon|Cult of the Dragon]]

**Enemies:** Towns and settlements targeted for raids (including [[Greenest]]); followers of [[Bahamut]] or any deity opposed to chromatic dragons

**Notes:** They appear to have a temporary camp roughly four hours south of [[Greenest]]; raids are coordinated and involve both humanoid cultists and draconic followers
