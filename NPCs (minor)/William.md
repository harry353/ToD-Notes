**Race/Class:** Roper  
**Location:** Cave beneath the raider camp, in the large cavern housing the black dragon eggs  
**Faction:** None; independent, working with the [[Cult of the Dragon]] for food  
**Notes:** Originally inhabited the cave before the cult arrived. Guards the black dragon eggs in exchange for being fed by the cultists. Killed [[Edgewyn]]'s bat scout. The party left him alive, fed him the body of [[Cyanwrath]], and persuaded him to eat the dragons once they hatch.
