## Session Summary

The party follows the tracks left by the raiders heading south. After three hours, the terrain turns rocky, and they spot two [[Kobolds|kobold]] camps that appear distracted. Choosing to avoid confrontation, they circle around them and continue along the path.

A little farther ahead, on a narrow trail surrounded by cliffsides, [[Piiinuz]] notices a face watching from a rock about twenty feet away. As the party approaches the spot, everyone except Edgewyn is caught in a rolling rock trap, Indiana-Jones style. Their attackers then reveal themselves as members of the [[Cult of the Dragon]].

Combat breaks out. After a hard fight, only the leader remains standing. A *sleep* spell brings him down, and the party ties him up.
