Wednesday, 4th of March, 2026

## Session Summary

The party observed three prisoners on posts and five others shackled to the dirt, including a barely conscious [[Leosin Erlanthar|Leosin]] and the [[Blonde prisoner]], being tortured by 2 [[kobolds]]. Four humanoid guards stood outside a nearby cave, while another four humanoids and four guard drakes protected a large tent. Nearby, huts were marked with crude animal and dragon signs.

In an attempt to gather more information, the party tries to mingle some more with the crowd. [[Garus]] joined a group of dancing [[kobolds]] to blend in, performing "the worm" after slipping to win them over. The party claimed they were new recruits and asked about [[Frulam Mondath|F.M.]], which initially raised suspicion. However, they diverted the conversation, though eventually did agree to meet one of the leaders. [[Piiinuz]] hid her bubblegun flamethrower in some nearby shrubs, just in case a distraction was needed to aid in a potential emergency escape. One of the kobolds entered the large tent and returned with a sober female humanoid in purple robes, armed with a halberd and sporting short black hair, identifying herself as the [[Frulam Mondath|author of the note]]. She questioned how the party knew her, and though [[Garus]] attempted to lie, he was caught. 

[[Piiinuz]] then activated her flamethrower, igniting a nearby tent. As the leader was distracted, the party feigned an attempt to help by moving toward a lake 30 feet away, which was close to where the prisoners were kept. [[Edgewyn]] cast _Fog Cloud_ over the prisoners while the party moved to untie them. In the obscured area, [[Garus]] accidentally struck [[Leosin Erlanthar|Leosin]] while trying to cut his bonds, but successfully freed the staked prisoners. [[Edgewyn]] freed a second captive from a cross, though [[Garus]] accidentally killed a third prisoner during the chaos. In total, 7 of the 8 prisoners were freed.

## Key Events

- While scouting the camp’s heavy guard presence, the party joined a group of [[kobolds]] to gather information before being confronted by a purple-robed female commander who identified herself as the author of the note signed as [[Frulam Mondath|F.M.]].
- After the leader caught [[Garus]] in a lie, [[Piiinuz]] triggered her hidden flamethrower to ignite a nearby tent, providing a diversion for the party to move toward the prisoners under the cover of a _Fog Cloud_ cast by [[Edgewyn]].
- During the chaotic rescue, the party successfully freed 7 of the 8 prisoners, including [[Leosin Erlanthar|Leosin]] himself, though [[Garus]] accidentally injured [[Leosin Erlanthar|Leosin]] and killed one other captive while cutting their bonds in the fog.
