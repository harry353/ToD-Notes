## Session Summary

The party breaks for the camp exit while the [[kobolds]] and [[Frulam Mondath]] are distracted by the fire. [[Garus]] helps [[Leosin Erlanthar]] move, but the group is spotted almost immediately, everyone except [[Edgewyn]]. Garus trips and goes down with Leosin, drawing the attention of all four ambush drakes near the main tent. [[Piiinuz]], spotted by Frulam, ducks behind a hut and urges nearby kobolds to help with the fire, adding to the chaos.

Garus crawls toward the exit dragging Leosin while [[Thora]], Edgewyn, and the prisoners follow, managing to slip past the main tent undetected. Ahead, Piiinuz, now in kobold disguise, talks the only conscious kobold in a nearby group into stepping into a hut, clearing the way. She is then given a handful of fleas by Edgewyn's, playing the part of a fan of his. The watchtower kobolds, distracted by the alarm bell and the fire, don't notice them approaching. The party makes it out and retreats to their earlier alcove, where the disguised kobold who followed them reveals herself as Piiinuz and her true nature as a changeling.

The party heads to [[Greenest]] with Leosin and the freed prisoners. Along the way, Leosin shares what he knows. The Greenest attack was the largest the cult has mounted so far. Cultists were hauling large game into the cave at the back of the camp, likely to feed something inside. [[Frulam Mondath|Furlam]] handles day-to-day operations, [[Cyanwrath]] was also there, in the red tent, and [[Rezmir]], the highest-ranking officer, hasn't been seen in four days, leaving the two of them in charge. The cult is organized around the five chromatic heads of [[Tiamat]], with the faction that attacked Greenest corresponding to the black head, based on the color of Rezmir's mask. Leosin adds that the [[Harpers]] are working to prevent Tiamat's return, and that the new archdevil of [[Avernus]] is proving far more unpredictable than his predecessor.

Back in Greenest, the party is welcomed warmly. [[Eadyan Falconmoon]] tends to the wounded and [[Governor Nighthill]] pays their reward. Leosin and [[Nesim Waladra]] plan to travel north to [[Baldur's Gate]] to share their findings. He expects the camp to relocate soon and suggests the party investigate the cave after it does, promising to pay them when they meet again in Baldur's Gate.
