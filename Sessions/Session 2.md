Wednesday, November 12

## Session Summary

[[Garus]] notices that, despite the roar of the fire, there’s still fighting near the [[Greenest Keep|keep]], with more groups of raiders around and many townsfolk fleeing there for protection. He questions the captured [[Kobolds|kobold]], who says that a certain [[Tiamat|Tiamat]] ordered them to collect “shinies” and that their camp lies south of town.

The group formally introduces themselves. When the [[Kobolds|kobold]] speaks up again, [[Edgewyn]] hits it with _mage hand_, killing it. [[Garus]] and [[Thora]] try to break [[Piiinuz]]’s manacles by force but fail, but [[Edgewyn]] easily picks the lock. [[Piiinuz]] loots all the daggers from the dead [[Kobolds|kobolds]].

The party heads toward the [[Greenest Keep|keep]] in the center of town. Along the way, they see more raiders breaking into homes and looting. They make it inside the keep just as the guards are closing the gates. Among the survivors is a [[Governor Nighthill|figure]], shouting orders from the parapets despite injuries to his head and arm. [[Thora]] approaches him to ask how they can help. He introduces himself as [[Governor Nighthill]] and explains that scouts have reported two urgent situations: in the southeast of [[Greenest]], at the [[Temple of Chauntea]], dozens of [[The Temple of Chauntea|townsfolk are trapped]] inside while raiders try to break in; and in the southwest, more raiders are attacking the [[The Greenest Mill|town’s mill]], threatening the food supply for winter. The party decides to go to the [[Temple of Chauntea|temple]], and [[Governor Nighthill|Nighthill]] gives them two healing potions.

Since the keep is surrounded, the group uses a secret tunnel that leads out to the river. They are given the keys and make their way down through a narrow stairway to a locked metal gate, which they unlock. Deeper in, they hear the sound of rats ahead. [[Edgewyn]] looks around a corner and spots two nests of rat swarms. He tosses some rations to distract them long enough for everyone to slip past.

At the tunnel’s end, [[Garus]] oils the rusty door so [[Thora]] can unlock it. [[Thora]] emerges first and spots a small group of raiders on the riverbank—two hooded humans and six [[Kobolds|kobolds]]. The group fails to stay quiet, alerting one of the humans, who appears to be a man in his thirties. A fight breaks out, and when it’s over, one of the humans is left unconscious for interrogation.

## Key Events

- The captured [[Kobolds|kobold]] reveals that a [[Tiamat]] leads the raiders and that their camp is south of town; the party then frees [[Piiinuz]] from her manacles and heads toward the [[Greenest Keep|keep]].
- They reach the keep as it closes, meet the injured [[Governor Nighthill]], and learn about two crises: townsfolk trapped in the [[The Temple of Chauntea|Temple of Chauntea]] and raiders attacking the [[The Greenest Mill|mill]].
- Choosing the temple, they take a secret tunnel under the keep, slipping past rat swarms after distracting them with rations.
- At the tunnel exit, they encounter a group of raiders, fail to stay hidden, and defeat them, leaving one human alive for questioning.
