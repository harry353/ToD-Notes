## Session Summary

The party fights off five kobolds, one winged, along with three ambush drakes. Mid-fight, [[Garus]] notices he feels stronger compared to before his death, and says so out loud, unsettling the rest of the party. [[Thora]] tries to read him with an insight check, but gets nothing.

The party heads down another set of stairs and encounters a thick curtain crudely made of layered animal skins blocking a doorway. They push through anyway, triggering a hidden poison barb trap that hits Garus, who responds by slicing the curtain in half. Beyond it is a chamber reeking of old blood, the floor stained dark, with four columns connected by chains hung with animal carcasses. The air here is noticeably colder than the rest of the dungeon. Finding nothing else of note, the party backtracks to investigate where the earlier kobolds had come from.

[[Thora]] steps on a tarp hiding nails and takes damage. The party finds a training room with dummies and a rack of wooden weapons, then moves to the last unexplored area. Garus sets off another trap, dropping rocks on him, [[Piiinuz]], and Thora. They find a room with straw beds, rats and lizards picking at food scraps, and moldy wineskins on the floor. Searching turns up a handful of coins and several crude, non-magical dragon talismans and charms.

A final staircase leads further down, the path dipping and then rising again until the party spots light ahead, about 60 feet away. Following it, they find pale moonlight filtering through shrubbery at the dungeon's exit.

Before leaving, the party debates dealing with the dragon eggs but decides to leave them alone, choosing instead to feed [[Cyanwrath]]'s body to [[William]]. While retrieving his armor, [[Garus]] finds a tattoo between his shoulder blades, a five-pointed star with bent spikes, a symbol of [[Tiamat]]. They feed the body to William and convince him to eat the dragons once they hatch, then exit the cave to find themselves overlooking the same camp from before.

The party returns to [[Greenest]] to report to [[Governor Nighthill]], who is glad to see them and receives their information on the cult's planned attacks and the dragon eggs. He's disappointed the eggs were left intact but understands the decision. He shares that [[Leosin Erlanthar]] and [[Nesim Waladra]] have departed for [[Baldur's Gate]], and that the party should seek him out there. The monk and his student went northeast instead, passing through [[Elturel]] first. The party spends the night in Greenest, planning to head for Elturel the next day.
