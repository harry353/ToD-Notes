## Session Summary

The party returns to the camp to investigate what the raiders left behind. The fire has been put out, with only wisps of smoke remaining, and about a third of the camp is burnt. Some movement is visible near the watchtowers: a group of hunters eating venison on a spit, dressed differently from the cult. The party approaches and confirms they are just hunters sharing a meal. [[Piiinuz]] buys a bowl of deer meat for a silver coin, as well as some of Steve's [[Steve's Chili Oil|chili oil]]. The hunters mention they were hired by [[Frulam Mondath]] to bring game, and that some humanoids, drakes, and kobolds may still be around or inside the cave.

The party heads to the cave. [[Garus]]'s shiny armor is not helping with stealth. [[Piiinuz]] casts *Detect Magic*. Inside, they find clusters of purple mushrooms, which [[Thora]] vaguely recalls hearing that such mushrooms are living creatures that won't react unless disturbed. Stairs lead further down at the back of the cave, surrounded by more mushrooms, alongside a secondary passage. The party takes the passage and finds two rooms: one full of barrels and storage, and one with two guards.

Thora tries to bluff the guards by claiming the party is part of the hunting group, but they see through it and raise the alarm, calling seven more guards. The party dispatches all of them quickly. Listening for reinforcements, they hear loud snoring coming from the storage room. [[Garus]] and [[Piiinuz]] investigate and find a drunk cultist asleep on the ground among mostly empty crates and scattered coins. Garus tries to roll him over and search him but fails at both.

The party moves into a room adjacent to the guards' room containing rugs, desks, tables, and papers. [[Thora]] finds a map marked with Xs over various villages, including [[Greenest]], with an arrow pointing west toward [[Beregost]] and another heading north along the main road. A separate note written in Common reads that everything is to be freighted north to [[Nevitar]], and that [[Rezmir]] is allowing them to keep some pearls, a ring, and a handful of gems. The remaining papers are unremarkable, some containing bad poetry about dragons.

In an adjacent room the party finds two chests, one with plain clothing and one with purple formal regalia and a key. [[Edgewyn]], while looking around, steps on a hidden pressure plate beneath a carpet, revealing a secret passage. He descends first into a chamber with unnaturally smooth walls and floor, warm and humid air, and shallow abstract drawings on the walls. In the northwest corner stands a large stone statue of a five-headed dragon rising from an erupting volcano, with smaller dragons gathering around it. At its base sits a chest, and before it a table repurposed as an altar, covered in candles and offerings. Standing at the altar are [[Cyanwrath]], [[Frulam Mondath]], and a handful of cultists.
