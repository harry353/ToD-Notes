**Status:** Active  
**Objective:** Reach the [[Temple of Chauntea]], rescue the trapped townsfolk, and escort them safely back to the [[Greenest Keep]].  
**Involved NPCs:** [[Governor Nighthill]], townsfolk of [[Greenest]], [[Kobolds|kobold]] raiders, [[Dragon Lady]]
**Locations:** [[Greenest]], [[Greenest Keep]], [[Temple of Chauntea]], southern farmlands near the river  
**Notes:** The keep is surrounded, so the party must use the secret tunnel leading to the river to exit unnoticed. Raiders and [[Kobolds|kobolds]] continue looting throughout the town, so the party has to be careful about making it to the temple and back. The party may need to choose between this mission and protecting the [[The Greenest Mill|Greenest mill]] due to time constraints.
