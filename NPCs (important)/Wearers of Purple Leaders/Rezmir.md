### Rezmir

**Race/Class/Gender:** Black half-dragon 
**Location:** Unknown; not seen at the raider camp for at least four days as of [[Session 9]] 
**Faction/Position/Rank:** Highest-ranking officer at the raider camp; leader within the [[Cult of the Dragon|Wearers of Purple]] 
**Appearance:** Distinctive black dragon-like features; typically wears a black mask 
**Notes:** Identified as a primary leader gathering a hoard to herald the return of [[Tiamat]]. With Rezmir absent, [[Frulam Mondath]] and [[Cyanwrath]] have been left in charge of camp operations. The black mask she wears suggests the Greenest faction corresponds to the black head of Tiamat.
